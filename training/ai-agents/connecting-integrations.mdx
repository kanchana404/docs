## Introduction

Now that you have created a new business, the next crucial step is to set it up with the necessary integrations to make it fully functional. This involves connecting communication channels so your AI agents can interact with contacts, and linking your AI providers to power their conversations.

## Step 1: Navigate to Business Settings

All integrations are managed on a per-business basis. This allows you to use different phone numbers, communication apps, or AI keys for each client or department.

1. From the left-hand navigation menu, ensure you have the correct business selected in the dropdown at the top.
2. Click on the **"Settings"** tab at the bottom of the menu. This will take you to the **Integrations** page.

## Step 2: Connect Your Communication Channels

For an AI agent to send or receive messages (like SMS, iMessage, or Instagram DMs), you must first connect a communication service.

- **Primary Options:** We recommend connecting either **GoHighLevel** or **Twilio**.
  - **GoHighLevel:** A comprehensive platform that can manage your entire CRM, automated follow-ups, and multiple communication channels.
  - **Twilio:** A direct integration that gives you access to voice calls, SMS, WhatsApp, and email.
- **Other Channels:** We are continuously expanding our native integrations. Keep an eye out for direct connections to **iMessage**, **WhatsApp**, **Instagram**, **Facebook Messenger**, and more.

You can filter the available integrations by clicking the **"Communication"** tab at the top of the page.

## Step 3: Connect Your AI Providers

To give your agents their "brain," you must connect at least one AI provider.

1. While on the **Integrations** page, click the **"AI"** filter tab.
2. **Connect OpenAI (Required):**
   - Click the **"Connect with Business API Key"** button under the OpenAI option.
   - A pop-up window will appear. Paste your OpenAI API key into the field.
   - Click the link in the pop-up if you need help finding your API key on the OpenAI platform.
   - Click **"Update API Key"**.
3. **Connect Claude (Anthropic) (Optional but Recommended):**
   - We highly recommend also connecting your Claude API key.
   - Agent Kongai uses Claude as an automatic backup. If OpenAI's servers experience downtime or rate-limit errors, our system will seamlessly switch to Claude to ensure your agents continue to function without interruption.

**⚠️ *CRITICAL: Enable Auto-Billing on Your AI Account***

- It is extremely important that you enable **auto-billing** or **auto-recharge** for your credits within your OpenAI and/or Anthropic accounts.
- If your account runs out of credits, **your AI agents will immediately stop working** and will not be able to respond to messages. Enabling auto-billing prevents service interruptions and ensures your agents are always online to capture leads and book appointments.
