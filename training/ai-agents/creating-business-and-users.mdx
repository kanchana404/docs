## I) Introduction

Welcome to your Agent Kongai tutorial. In this guide, you will learn the fundamentals of setting up your account, including how to create and manage multiple businesses and how to add and configure new users with different permission levels.

## II) Understanding the Control Center

The **Control Center** is the administrative backend of your Agent Kongai account. It's the central hub where you can manage every aspect of your operations, including all of your businesses and users.

You can access it from the top of the left-hand navigation menu.

## III) Creating a New Business

Agent Kongai allows you to create and manage an unlimited number of businesses from a single account. This is perfect for agencies managing multiple clients or for companies with distinct departments. There are two primary ways to create a new business.

### Method 1: From the Control Center

1. Navigate to the **Control Center**.
2. Click on the **"Businesses"** tab in the left-hand menu.
3. Click the blue **"+ Add Business"** button in the top-right corner.
4. A "Create a new Business" window will appear. Fill in the following details:
   - **Business Name:** Enter a name for the new business.
   - **Who Owns This Business?:** Select the appropriate ownership status.
   - **Industry:** Choose the relevant industry from the dropdown menu.
5. Click **"Create Business"**. You will be taken directly to the dashboard of your newly created business.

### Method 2: Using the Quick Add Button

For faster creation without having to navigate back to the Control Center every time:

1. Click the business selector dropdown menu at the top of the left-hand navigation bar.
2. At the bottom of this menu, click the **"+ Add Business"** button.
3. Fill out the business details in the pop-up window, just as in Method 1.
4. Click **"Create Business"**.

## IV) Managing Users and Permissions

You can add team members or clients to your account and control which businesses they have access to.

### Step 1: Creating a New User

1. Navigate to the **Control Center**.
2. Click on the **"Users"** tab in the left-hand menu.
3. Click the **"+ Add New User"** button in the top-right corner.
4. Enter the user's details:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Password
5. Select an **Access Level** for the user.
6. Click **"Create"**.

**Understanding Access Levels**

- **Manager:** A Manager can only access and manage the businesses that are *specifically assigned* to them. This is the ideal role for team members or clients who only need access to certain projects.
- **Admin:** An Admin has full access to *all businesses* within your account. This role should be reserved for account owners or high-level administrators.

### Step 2: Assigning Businesses to a Manager

If you have set a user's Access Level to "Manager," you must assign them to the businesses you want them to access.

1. From the **"Users"** tab in the Control Center, click the **pencil icon** next to the user you wish to edit.
2. On the User Details page, you will see a section for **"Assigned Businesses"**. Click the **"+ Assign"** button.
3. A pop-up window will appear. Check the boxes next to all the businesses you want this user to access.
4. Click the **"Assign"** button. The user will now only have access to the selected businesses.

**💡 Pro Tip:** You can also add users directly to a specific business. Navigate to the business's dashboard, click on **"Settings"** in the left menu, go to the **"Users"** tab, and click **"+ Add User"**. Any user created this way will automatically be assigned to that specific business.
