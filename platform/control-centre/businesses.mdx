## Managing Your Businesses in the Control Centre

**Introduction**

The **Businesses** page within the AgentKong Control Centre is your primary dashboard for overseeing all the separate business accounts you manage. Whether you're running different companies, managing client accounts, or organizing various internal projects, this is where you will add, edit, and organize them. This guide will walk you through the features of this page and how to use them effectively.

## Understanding the Businesses Page

This page provides a clear, at-a-glance table of all your business accounts.

### Key Features & Columns:

- **Search businesses:** Quickly find a specific account by typing its name in the search bar.
- **Business List:** A sortable list of all your accounts. The columns include:
- **Bulk Actions:** By using the checkboxes next to each business name (or the main checkbox at the top), you can select multiple businesses at once to perform a **bulk delete**.

## Adding a New Business

You can create a new business account from two locations on this page:

1. Click the **"+ Add Business"** button in the top-right corner.
2. Click the Business selector in the top left corner and click the **"+ Add Business"** button at the bottom.

## Editing an Existing Business

1. Locate the business you want to modify in the list.
2. Click the **Edit** icon (pencil) in the "Actions" column.
3. In the "Edit Business" pop-up, you can update the Business Name, reassign the owner, or change the industry.
4. Click **"Update Business"** to save your changes.

**Important Note on Plans:** AgentKong does not limit the number of *businesses* you can create. However, your subscription plan may have limits on the total number of *AI agents* you can create across all your businesses.
