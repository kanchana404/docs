## The Settings Page

The "Settings" page is your central hub for configuring your entire AgentKong account. This is where you manage your business information, subscriptions, and usage across all products.

# Account Tab: Business Information

This is where you can manage the basic information for your main account.

**Key Concept:** The "Company Name" set here represents your primary business or agency, not the individual client businesses you might manage within the platform.

**Instructions:**

1. Navigate to **Settings** from the main menu.
2. You will land on the **Account** tab by default.
3. In the "Company Name" field, enter or update your business's name.
4. Click the **Save Changes** button to apply.

# Billing Tab

This tab provides a complete overview of your subscriptions and allows you to manage all billing-related details, including products, payment methods, and history.

### Your Products

Manage your subscriptions to individual AgentKong products like AI Agents and Ad Tracking.

**Key Concept:** You can subscribe to different products and select specific tiers for each one based on your needs, allowing for flexible and scalable billing.

**Instructions:**

1. While in Settings, click on the **Billing** tab.
2. Under the "Products" section, you can view your currently subscribed products, their plans, and monthly costs.
3. **To change your plan:**
   - Click the **Update** button next to the product you wish to modify (e.g., AI Agents).
   - A pop-up will display the available plans (e.g., 1 Agent, 10 Agents, Unlimited Agents).
   - Select your desired plan and click **Update Plan**.
4. **Downgrading a Plan:**
   - If you downgrade to a plan with a lower limit (e.g., from Unlimited to 1 Agent), a "Downgrade Requires Action" window will appear.
   - This screen will require you to deactivate the necessary number of active agents to meet the new plan's limit.
   - Toggle off agents until the requirement is met, then click **Confirm Downgrade**.
5. **To add a new product subscription:**
   - Click the **+ Add Product** button to subscribe to any available products you are not currently using.

### Payment & History

1. Click the **Payment** sub-tab to add, remove, or set a default payment method for your account.
2. Click the **History** sub-tab to view your billing history and download past invoices for your records.

# Usage Tab

This tab gives you a detailed look at which specific services are currently active, tying directly back to your billing plan and limits.

**Key Concept:** This is the best place to quickly see which AI Agents or Ad Tracking instances are active and counting towards your subscription quota, which is essential before making any plan changes.

**Instructions:**

1. While in Settings, click on the **Usage** tab.
2. **AI Agent Management:**
   - Click the "AI Agent" sub-tab.
   - Here you will see a list of all your AI agents, the business they belong to, their creation date, and their current **Status** (Active/Inactive).
3. **Ad Tracking Management:**
   - Click the "Ad Tracking" sub-tab.
   - This shows a list of all businesses and whether Ad Tracking is **Enabled** or **Disabled** for each one.

# Navigating the Bottom-Left Menu

The menu at the bottom-left corner contains several useful tools for managing your profile and accessing other platform features.

### Earn 40% Commission*

AgentKong offers a referral program. This link gives you quick access to your unique affiliate link and portal.

**Instructions:**

1. Locate and click on **40% Commission*** at the bottom of the left-hand menu.
2. This will either navigate you to the full Affiliate Portal or instantly copy your personal referral link to your clipboard.

### My Profile

This menu allows you to manage your personal user details or log out of the platform.

**Instructions:**

1. Click on your **Profile icon/name** at the bottom of the menu.
2. A small menu will appear with two options:
   - **Edit Profile**: This will take you to the "User Details" page, where you can see your name, email, and access level. From there, you can edit your information or reset your password.
   - **Log out**: This will securely log you out of your AgentKong account.

### Dark Mode / Light Mode

Easily switch the platform's appearance to suit your preference.

**Instructions:**

1. At the very bottom of the left-hand menu, click the **Dark Mode** or **Light Mode** toggle (often represented by a sun or moon icon).
2. The interface will immediately switch to your selected theme.
