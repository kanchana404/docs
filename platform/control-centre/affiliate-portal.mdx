## Introduction to the AgentKong Affiliate Portal

Welcome to the AgentKong Affiliate Program! We believe in sharing our success with our community, which is why every AgentKong user is automatically enrolled in our generous affiliate program. This portal is your central hub for tracking your referrals, monitoring your commissions, and accessing your unique referral link. You can earn up to 40% lifetime commission just by sharing AgentKong with others.

This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started and maximize your earnings.

## Accessing Your Affiliate Portal

When you create an account with AgentKong, an affiliate account is automatically generated for you.

1. Navigate to the **Control Centre** using the main menu on the left.
2. Click on **Affiliate Portal**. This will take you to your main affiliate dashboard where you can manage everything related to the program.

You can also quickly access your referral link at any time by clicking the **"Earn 40% Commission"** button at the bottom of the left-hand menu, no matter where you are in the application.

## Your Affiliate Dashboard Explained

The affiliate dashboard provides a complete overview of your performance and earnings.

## Key Metrics

At the top of the portal, you'll find a summary of your earnings:

- **Due in X Days:** Shows upcoming commission payments.
- **Total Unpaid:** The total amount of commission you've earned that has not yet been paid out.
- **Total Paid:** The total amount of commission you have received to date.

## Referral Links

This section is where you'll find and manage your unique link to share with others.

- **Your Default Link:** A unique referral link is automatically created for you.
- **Customize Token:** You can personalize your link by clicking **Customize Token**. This allows you to change the identifier at the end of the URL to something more memorable.
- **New Link:** This feature allows you to create additional, unique links. This is for tracking the performance of different campaigns or promotional channels (e.g., one link for your YouTube channel and another for your blog).

## Understanding Your Commission Payout

We've designed a tiered commission structure to reward our most active affiliates.

- **Initial Tier (20% Commission):** For your first two successful referrals, you will earn a 20% lifetime recurring commission on their subscriptions.
- **Gold Tier (40% Commission):** After your first two referrals, your commission rate permanently increases to **40% for life** on all subsequent referrals.

**Gold Tier Perks:**
Once you reach the Gold Tier, you unlock exclusive benefits, including:

- Access to an exclusive affiliate community.
- Bonus perks and direct access to the AgentKong founding team.
- Opportunities for joint ventures, such as webinars for your community.

## Managing Payouts and Reporting

The navigation bar at the top of the portal helps you manage the specifics of your account.

## Setting Up Payouts

To receive your commissions, you must set up a payout method.

1. From the home dashboard, click the **Select Payout Method** button.
2. You will be prompted to select a method. Currently, **PayPal** is supported.
3. Enter your PayPal email address and click **Add**. All commissions will be sent to this account.

## Navigating Your Portal

- **Home:** Your main dashboard overview.
- **Referrals:** A detailed list of everyone who has signed up using your link.
- **Commissions:** A transparent breakdown of all commissions you have earned.
- **Payouts:** A history of all payments sent to you. You can also manage your payout terms and methods here.
- **Assets:** This section will be updated with promotional materials like PDFs, videos, and slideshows to help you promote AgentKong.
- **Reports:** An in-depth look at your performance, including earnings, clicks, signups, and traffic sources over specific timeframes.
