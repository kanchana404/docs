The White Label Settings page allows you to fully brand your AgentKong platform, replacing the default branding with your own custom logo and domain. This ensures your clients see your product as 100% your brand.

## 1. Navigation

To access the White Label Settings:

1. Navigate to the **Control Center** (use the drop-down menu in the top left if you are currently in a business's dashboard).
2. In the left sidebar menu, click on **White Label**.

## 2. Logo Management

You can upload separate logos for **Light Mode** and **Dark Mode** to ensure visibility across all user interface themes. If you only upload one logo, it will be used for both modes.

## Step-by-Step: Uploading Your Logo

1. Locate the **Light Mode Logo** section.
2. Click the **Choose File** button or drag and drop your logo file into the upload area.
3. Wait for the file to upload and the logo preview to appear.
4. (Optional, but recommended) Repeat steps 1-3 for the **Dark Mode Logo** with a logo optimized for a dark background (e.g., a white or light-colored version of your logo).

## 3. Custom Domain Setup

To completely white label your platform, you must set up a custom subdomain (e.g., app.yourbusiness.com). This ensures your clients access the platform through your branded domain instead of agentkong.com.

## Domain Requirements

- Only **subdomains** are supported (e.g., app.yourclient.com).
- **Root domains** (e.g., yourclient.com) are **not** supported.
- You must have access to your domain's **DNS settings** to complete the setup.

## Step-by-Step: Adding and Verifying Your Custom Domain

1. In the **Custom Domain** field, enter your desired subdomain (e.g., app.mywebsite.com or myai.mywebsite.com).
2. Click the **Add Domain** button.
3. The system will generate a list of required DNS records (typically CNAME and/or TXT records) that you need to add to your domain registrar (e.g., GoDaddy, Namecheap, Cloudflare, etc.).
4. **Access your Domain Provider's DNS Settings:** Log in to your domain registrar's control panel.
5. **Add the Required DNS Records:** Create the records (e.g., CNAME, TXT) exactly as specified by the AgentKong platform.
   - *Tip: Search YouTube or your domain provider's help center for specific instructions on "how to add DNS records to [your domain provider]".*
6. **Verify the Domain:** Return to the White Label Settings page and click the **Verify** button (this button will appear after you attempt to add the domain). Once the DNS records have propagated (this can take a few minutes to several hours), the domain will be verified.

## 4. Why White Label?

White Labeling your platform provides significant business advantages:

- **Your Brand Identity:** Your clients see YOUR brand—not AgentKong. Your logo appears everywhere in the platform.
- **Custom Access:** Clients access the platform through your domain (e.g., app.yourbusiness.com) instead of agentkong.com.
- **Trust and Retention:** Clients think this is software you built, increasing trust and retention.
- **Premium Positioning:** Allows you to charge more, positioning the platform as your own custom and premium product.
