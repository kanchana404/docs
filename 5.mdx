    # URL Parameters for Meta Ads

    Setting up URL parameters for your Meta ads is a straightforward process, but it’s the most critical step for accurate attribution. Here is how to get them installed and verified.

    ### 1. Adding Parameters to a Single Ad

    To get started, navigate to the **Ad** level in your Meta Ads Manager.

    1. Scroll down to the **Tracking** section.
    2. Locate the **URL parameters** field.
    3. Copy the specific parameter string provided in your dashboard and paste it directly into this box.

    ### 2. Bulk Editing Multiple Ads

    If you have dozens of ads to update, you don't need to do them one by one.

    1. Select all the ads you want to update from the Ads Manager list.
    2. Click **Edit**.
    3. Scroll down to the **Tracking** section and paste your URL parameters into the field.
    4. Hit **Publish**.

    <Warning>
      **Note on Publishing:** When you hit publish, Meta will momentarily pause your ads while they review the changes. This is a standard small change, so ads are typically back up and running within 5 to 15 minutes.
    </Warning>

    ### 3. Verifying Your Setup

    It's important to keep an eye on your tracking status to ensure everything is configured properly. There are two main ways to do this:

    ### In Meta Ads Manager

    I recommend customizing your columns in Ads Manager to include **URL parameters**. This allows you to visibly confirm at a glance which ads have the tracking code installed and which do not.

    ### In Your Attribution Dashboard

    Inside your tracking table, look for the **Tracking** column. This acts as a "root check" for your setup:

    - **Green Checkmark:** You’re good to go! The URL parameters are configured properly.
    - **Red Triangle:** There is an error in the string.
    - **Red X:** Parameters are missing entirely.

    <Tip>
      If you see anything other than a green checkmark, simply click the icon to see exactly what is missing or misconfigured so you can fix it quickly.
    </Tip>

    ```jsx
    utm_source=Meta+Ads&utm_medium={{adset.name}}&utm_campaign={{campaign.name}}&utm_content={{ad.name}}&campaign_id={{campaign.id}}&medium_id={{adset.id}}&content_id={{ad.id}}
    ```

    > NOTE: we are backwards compatible with Hyros URL Parameters

  # URL Parameters for Google Ads

  Adding URL parameters to your Google Ads account is quite simple. Whether you're updating your existing campaigns or starting a fresh one, here’s how to get your tracking set up.

  ### For Existing Campaigns

  If you already have Google ads running, follow these steps to bulk update your tracking:

  1. Navigate to **Campaigns** in your Google Ads dashboard.
  2. Click the **Settings** tab.
  3. Select all the campaigns you want to track.
  4. Click the **Edit** button and select **Change tracking templates**.
  5. Copy and paste the URL parameters below in your dashboard into the **New tracking template** field.
  6. Hit **Apply**.

  <Note>
    ```text wrap
    {lpurl}?campaign_id={campaignid}&medium_id={adgroupid}&ad_id={creative}&utm_keyword={keyword}
    ```
  </Note>

  ### For New Campaigns

  If you’re creating a new campaign from scratch, you can set your tracking template during the setup process:

  1. Start your new campaign creation.
  2. Navigate to the **Campaign settings** section.
  3. Scroll to the bottom, click **More settings**, and then select **Campaign URL options**.
  4. Paste your tracking code into the **Tracking template** field.
  5. Click **Next** and continue with your campaign build—away you go!

  <Tip>
    Setting your tracking at the campaign level ensures that all ads within that campaign automatically inherit the correct URL parameters, saving you time and preventing manual errors.
  </Tip>
