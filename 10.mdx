# Getting Started with the Cortana AI Extension

Alright, if you’re looking to get the Cortana AI Chrome extension up and running, follow these steps to integrate your conversion data directly into your Meta Ads Manager.

## Installation

Getting the extension into your browser is the first step to seeing your real conversion data where it matters.

<Steps><Step title="Find the Extension">Head over to the <span style={{color: '#1a73e8'}}>**[Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/cortana-ai/jkapahgdmjffdkconinemcpmkponpgfi)**</span> to find the Cortana AI extension directly.</Step><Step title="Add to Browser">Click **Add to Brave** (or Add to Chrome). When the permission pop-up appears, select **Add extension**.</Step><Step title="Pin for Easy Access">I always recommend pinning the extension to your toolbar. Click the puzzle icon in your browser, find Cortana AI, and hit the pin icon so it’s always visible at the top.</Step></Steps>

## Connecting to Ads Manager

Once installed, you need to link the extension to your Cortana AI account.

<Steps><Step title="Open Ads Manager">Navigate to your Meta Ads Manager tab.</Step><Step title="Sign In">Click the Cortana AI icon in your toolbar and select **Sign in with Cortana AI**. This will authenticate your session.</Step><Step title="Refresh">Refresh your Ads Manager page to initialize the Cortana AI overlay.</Step></Steps>

## Initial Onboarding

After refreshing, a quick onboarding wizard will appear to help you configure your view.

1. **Select Your Business:** Choose the specific business account you want to track.
2. **Choose a Column Preset:** Select the conversion configuration you want to display.
3. **Set Your Timezone:** Verify that your timezone matches your reporting requirements.
4. **Toggle Columns:** Use the toggle switches to enable or disable specific data columns in your view.

<Note>You can customize which data points are displayed at any time via the extension settings at the top of your Ads Manager.</Note>

## Troubleshooting Alignment Issues

Meta's interface can be a bit finicky with data overlays. If you ever see the rows shifting or data not aligning perfectly with the columns, it’s usually a spacing issue.

**The Fix:**

Simply hover your mouse over the column border (like the "Delivery" or "Budget" column) and **drag it to make the column wider**. Once there's enough room, the Cortana AI data will snap right back into place.

## Accessing Deep Insights

The extension doesn't just show numbers—it shows people.

- **View Contacts:** Click on any data point (like a "74" in the Appointments Booked column) to see a list of the specific names, emails, and phone numbers associated with those conversions.
- **Customer Journey:** Click on an individual contact to view their full customer journey, just like you would inside the main Cortana AI app.
