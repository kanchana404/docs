            # Editing Conversion Attribution

            Alright, so if you ever need to manually change the attribution for a conversion event, we've made it easy to do that from two different spots. Here's how you can get it done.

            ## From the Main Table

            If you're looking at your conversion events—like a Purchase—you can edit the attribution directly from the main list.

            <Steps>
              <Step title="Open the Conversion Modal">
                  Click into the event type you want to view (e.g., Purchase).
                    </Step>
                      <Step title="Locate the Attribution Column">
                          Find the specific conversion you want to change and click the pencil icon next to the attribution label.
                            </Step>
                              <Step title="Update Attribution Details">
                                  You have two ways to do this. <strong>Top-Down:</strong> Select a specific Ad — if you know the ad, selecting it will automatically resolve the Medium, Campaign, and Source for you. <strong>Bottom-Up:</strong> If you don't know the specific ad, start at the Source and work your way up to manually set the campaign and medium.
                                            </Step>
                                              <Step title="Save Changes">
                                                  Once you're happy with the changes, just hit **Save**.
                                                    </Step>
                                                    </Steps>

                                                    ## From the Webhook Data View

                                                    There's also another spot where you can make these changes if you're already deep-diving into the data.

                                                    <Steps>
                                                      <Step title="View Data">
                                                          Click on the **View Data** link on the far right of any conversion row.
                                                            </Step>
                                                              <Step title="Edit Attribution Data">
                                                                  In the **Webhook Data Details** window, find the **Attribution Data** section and click **Edit**.
                                                                    </Step>
                                                                      <Step title="Adjust and Save">
                                                                          This gives you the exact same ability to select an ad or manually adjust the source and campaign. Just click **Save** when you're finished.
                                                                            </Step>
                                                                            </Steps>

                                                                            <Note>**Pro Tip:** Selecting the specific Ad is the fastest way to edit attribution, as it auto-fills all the other tracking parameters for you!</Note>

                                                                            Hopefully, that makes sense! If you have any questions as you're working through this, please feel free to reach out to our team.
